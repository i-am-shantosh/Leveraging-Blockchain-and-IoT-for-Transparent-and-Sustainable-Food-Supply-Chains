Leveraging Blockchain and IoT for Transparent and Sustainable Food Supply Chains

Research Project | University of Peradeniya

Smart Tracking

Real-time monitoring of food transportation using IoT sensors to record temperature, humidity, and location.

Sustainability

Supports sustainable practices through transparency, reducing waste and optimizing resources.

Blockchain Security

Ensures data immutability and trust by leveraging blockchain technology for secure record keeping.

Objective 1

Design an end-to-end framework for food traceability using IoT and blockchain integration.

Objective 2

Implement decentralized data logging to prevent tampering and provide audit trails.

Objective 3

Analyze system scalability and performance under real-world scenarios and data loads.
